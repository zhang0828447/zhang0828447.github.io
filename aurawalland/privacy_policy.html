Effective: June 18, 2026 · Last updated: June 18, 2026

Welcome to AuraWall. We hope to help you understand what information we collect, why we collect it, how we use it, and your choices regarding it.

1. Information Collection

When you use our services, we may collect information to provide and improve our services. We may use the following third-party services:

  • Google Analytics for Firebase — to understand aggregate usage patterns and improve the app experience.
  • Firebase Crashlytics — to detect, diagnose, and fix crashes and stability issues.
  • AdMob — to display advertising within the app.

In case of errors, our systems may also collect log data, including your device's IP address, device name, operating system version, application configuration, usage date and time, and other statistical details.

2. Permissions Used by AuraWall

AuraWall requests only the permissions needed to deliver its core features. Each permission is described below.

  • INTERNET — to fetch online wallpaper content.
  • ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE — to detect connectivity and switch to cached content when you are offline or on a weak network.
  • SET_WALLPAPER — to apply a selected image as your home screen or lock screen wallpaper.
  • WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE (only on devices running Android 9 / API 28 or lower) — to save wallpapers to your local storage on older Android versions.

3. Custom Keyboard (Input Method)

AuraWall offers an optional custom keyboard theme. When enabled, Android may show a standard system warning reminding you that input methods can read what you type. AuraWall's keyboard service is used solely for displaying the visual keyboard theme and does not collect, store, or transmit the text you type. No personal input is sent to our servers.

4. Local Data

AuraWall stores data such as your favorited wallpapers, recently viewed items, keyboard theme preference, and page cache locally on your device to provide offline access and a faster experience. This data stays on your device and is not uploaded to us. AuraWall does not request, store, or transmit photos or videos from your gallery.

5. Use of Information

The information we collect is used solely to provide, maintain, and improve our services. We will not use or share your information except as described in this Privacy Policy. This helps us improve your experience and ensure the services function as intended.

6. Personal Information

To improve your experience, the application may require you to provide certain personal information. This information is stored on your device and is not collected directly by us.

7. Cookies

8. Service Providers

9. Security

10. Links to Other Websites

Our services may contain links to external websites. Clicking these links will take you to other websites. We recommend that you review the privacy policies of any websites you visit, as we have no control over their content or practices. We are not responsible for their privacy practices.
