Four proven techniques

Each one comes from medical or psychological research — for stress, sleep, focus, or energy.

4 — 7 — 8

4-7-8 Breathing

The "natural tranquilizer" popularized by Dr. Andrew Weil. Inhale 4, hold 7, exhale 8. A few rounds before bed helps you fall asleep faster.

4 — 4 — 4 — 4

Box Breathing

The technique used by Navy SEALs to stay calm under pressure. Four equal 4-second phases — perfect before a speech, exam, or difficult meeting.

5 — 5

Coherent Breathing

A balanced 5-second inhale and exhale that regulates heart rate variability. Ideal for everyday long-term practice.

Custom

Your Own Pattern

Different lungs, different habits. Set your own inhale, hold, and exhale durations to find the rhythm that suits you best.
