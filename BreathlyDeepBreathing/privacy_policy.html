Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 15, 2026

Welcome to Breathly. We hope to help you understand what information we collect, why we collect it, how we use it, and your choices regarding it.

1. Information Collection

When you use our services, we may collect information to provide and improve our services. All your app data — including your breathing session history, streak counts, custom breathing patterns, and favorites — is stored locally on your device and is not uploaded to any server. We may use the following third-party services:

  • Google Analytics for Firebase — collects anonymous usage statistics (such as app open events and crash reports) to help us improve the app.
  • Firebase Crashlytics — collects crash logs and diagnostic data to help us identify and fix issues.
  • Google AdMob — may use the Identifier for Advertisers (IDFA) to show you more relevant ads. You can limit ad tracking at any time in iPhone Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking.

In case of errors, our systems may also collect log data, including your device's IP address, device name, operating system version, and other statistical details.

2. Use of Information

The information we collect is used solely to provide, maintain, and improve our services. We will not use or share your information except as described in this Privacy Policy.

3. Personal Information

Breathly does not require account registration. Any information you provide (such as custom breathing patterns, favorites, and preferences) is stored locally on your device and is not collected directly by us.

4. Service Providers

We may collaborate with third-party companies to help provide and support our services. These third parties may access information as needed to perform their work but are not allowed to use it for other purposes.

5. Security

We value your trust and take commercially reasonable steps to protect your information. However, no method of online transmission or electronic storage is completely secure.

6. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Please check this page regularly for updates.
