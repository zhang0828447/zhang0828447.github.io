The information we collect is used solely to provide, maintain, and improve our services. We will not use or share your information except as described in this Privacy Policy. This helps us improve your experience and ensure the services function as intended.

To improve your experience, the application may ask you to provide certain personal information such as weight, activity level, daily goals, hydration logs, focus sessions, and mood entries. This information is stored locally on your device and is not collected directly by us.

4. Location Information
