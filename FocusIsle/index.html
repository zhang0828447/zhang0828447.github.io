Introducing FocusIsle

Stay focused.
Get things done.

A Pomodoro timer combined with task management, focus journal, and goal tracking. Everything you need to build lasting productivity habits.

Pomodoro Timer

Your flow,
your rules.

Focus Session, Short Break, or Long Break — switch freely. Pause and resume anytime. Skip breaks when you're on a roll, or enable continuous mode for unstoppable focus sessions. Customizable durations to match your rhythm.

Task Management

Organize everything.
One task at a time.

Group tasks into projects. Set priorities, due dates, and detailed notes. Build habits with recurring tasks. Break big goals into sub-tasks and checklists. Your to-do list, finally under control.

Focus Journal

Remember what
you accomplished.

After each focus session, jot down your thoughts and reflections. Over time, your focus diary becomes a powerful record of progress. Look back and see how far you've come — one session at a time.

Statistics & Goals

Daily, weekly, and monthly reports. Gantt chart and trend charts to see your progress. Calendar view for a quick overview. Set daily focus goals and track your streaks to stay motivated.
