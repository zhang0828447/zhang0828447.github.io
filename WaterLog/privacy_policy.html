WaterLog

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 11, 2026

1. Information Collection

WaterLog (hereinafter "the App") respects and protects your privacy. The App is a locally running water intake tracking tool designed with the principle of minimizing data collection.

Information We Collect:

  • Water Intake Records: Daily logged water amounts, beverage types, timestamps, and diary notes — all stored locally on your device
  • User Preferences: Daily goal, reminder times, unit preferences, city selection for weather — all stored locally on your device
  • Achievement Data: Level progress, streak counts, and goal completion history — all stored locally on your device

Information We Do NOT Collect:

  • Name, email, phone number, or other personal identity information
  • Geolocation data (weather data is obtained via IP-based city lookup or user-selected city)
  • Contacts, social media, photos, videos, or files
  • Health or fitness data
Core Commitment: The App does not require user account registration and does not collect any personal identification information. Your water intake data is fully under your control and stored locally on your device only.

3. Local Data Storage

All user data (water records, preferences, achievement progress) is stored locally on your device in a local database. No data is uploaded to any cloud server.

Important: Since data is stored locally only, uninstalling the app will result in permanent data loss that cannot be recovered.

4. Network Data Transmission

Core features (water reminders, intake logging, statistics) work completely offline. The network is only used for:

  • Weather Data: Fetching local temperature via a free weather API (Open-Meteo) for smart goal adjustments. Only IP address or city name is used for location lookup — no personal data is sent.
  • Ad Display: Google AdMob SDK for displaying ads
  • Anonymous Usage Analytics: Google Analytics for Firebase (anonymized)
  • Crash Reporting: Firebase Crashlytics for app stability improvement

Your water intake records and diary entries are never transmitted over the network.

6. Your Rights and Choices

  • Limit Ad Tracking: iPhone Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking
  • Manage Location: iPhone Settings > Privacy & Security > Location Services > WaterLog
  • Manage Notifications: iPhone Settings > Notifications > WaterLog
  • Delete Data: Long-press app icon > Remove App

7. Children's Privacy

Suitable for all ages. No personal information collected from children under 13. No registration, no social features.

8. Data Security

  • All data stored in the iOS app sandbox, inaccessible to other apps
  • Third-party SDK transmissions use HTTPS/TLS
  • No personal identification information stored on servers

9. Data Deletion

  • Uninstall: Long-press app icon > Remove App

All local data will be permanently deleted upon uninstall. Deletion is irreversible.
